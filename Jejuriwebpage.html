Jejuri

Jejuri is the taluka of city of pune district. Khandoba Mandir is an important hindu temple to the Hindu Lord Khandoba.khandoba ,one of the most visited in maharashtra .
Khandoba is an clan of God for many Maharashtrain castes and communities ,beloved as a god who grants wishes .

History

In 1739 Chimaji Appa, a general of the marathas empire and brother od Peshwa Bajirao, defeated the Portuguese in the Battle of Vasai.

Khandoba Temple

The Khandoba temple in Jejuri ,which lies to the southeast of PUNE in Maharashtra .The Temple is dedicated to Khandoba , also known as Mhalsakant or Malhari Martand or Mylaralinga.
